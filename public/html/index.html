Introduction to League of Legends

"League of Legends" (LoL) is a highly competitive, fast-paced action-strategy game designed for those who crave a hard-fought victory. It's a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) where players choose from a diverse cast of champions and collaborate with their team to push through enemy defenses and destroy the opposing team's nexus. Each game is a fresh challenge with new opportunities to devise strategies. It's free-to-play, with a constantly evolving roster of champions, each with their own unique abilities and roles within the team.
